Which is Best for Your Site?

🥇 Best Choice: Cream (#F5F1E8) - Most elegant, warm, luxury feel that perfectly complements gold. Perfect for event sites.
🥈 Runner-up: Champagne (#F7E7CE) - Slightly darker, more defined contrast, very premium appearance.
🥉 Alternative: Warm Beige (#E8DCC8) - If you want more contrast and a bolder look.
⭐ Modern: Light Ivory (#FFFFF0) - Almost white but with warmth, modern and clean.
✨ Sophisticated: Soft Taupe (#E8E0D5) - For a more muted, refined appearance.

💡 Tip: Remember to also update text colors from white to dark colors (like #0a0a0a or #1a1a1a) for readability on light backgrounds
